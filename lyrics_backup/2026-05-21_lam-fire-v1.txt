[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Akhiyan ch agg teri machdi, you know it's true
Seene vich dhadkan vadhdi, feeling brand new
Raat di haneri ch labhda haan, looking at you
Nasha tera sir te chadhda, making me move

[Hook 2]
Nachdi tu jadon mere saamne, stop the show
Husn tera mainu karda ae crazy, watch it glow
Main taan bas tera hi deewana haan, stealing my heart
Nede nede aaja mere sohniye, never apart

[Verse 1]
Ucchiyaan udaariyan main marda, touching the sky
Par teri ik jhalak layi marda, catching my eye
Kach de pyale vich dolda haan, jazbaat mera
Bheed vich kalla main khada haan, facing my fear
Tu vi taan takdi ae mainu, with that fire
Kadon aake bujhavengi sohniye, my only desire
Zindagi de rang phikke lagde, without your light
Apni bana laanga tenu, winning this fight

[Pre-Chorus]
Haneriyan raatan ch chamkave, your glowing skin
Dil di khamoshi ch shor mache, let me begin
Kinjh main dassa tenu haal mera, out of control
Saahan vich vasdi ae tu hi, taking my soul

[Chorus]
L.A.M. Fire wangu machdi ae tu, burning so bright
Tere piche aashiq main rulda haan, into the night
Ishqe da jaadu chalaundi ae, catching my gaze
Main taan sab bhul gaya tere layi, lost in your maze
Nakhra tera mainu lutda ae, making me weak
Tere bina hor na kujh disda, you're all I seek
Aaja hun bujha de pyas meri, holding it tight
L.A.M. Fire wangu machdi ae tu, my only light

[Verse 2]
Sadkaan te agg main launda haan, pushing the pace
Engine da shor mainu rokda na, winning the race
Kaali raatan vich tez main chalda haan, chasing your scent
Zehar wangu chadhdi ae tu mainu, 100 percent
Dil te nishane tu laundi ae, shooting me down
Hawaavan naal karda haan gallan main, spinning around
Ankhaan ch rohb mera bolda, setting the trend
Tenu main apni banawanga, to the very end

[Pre-Chorus]
Chup chap reh ke vi boldi ae, reading my mind
Duniya di bheed vichon labhda haan, leaving behind
Tenu main apni banawanga, make you my own
Ik vaari keh de tu meri ae, never alone

[Chorus]

[Verse 3]
Kadi kadi sochda haan kalla main, feeling the pain
Kyun aivein ishqe ch rull gaya, in the cold rain
Par jadon hasdi ae tu mainu dekh ke, melting the ice
Lagda ae jivein sab mil gaya, paying the price
Vairiyan naal lad jawan tere layi, taking the fall
Sajjna ve tenu main mangda haan, answering the call
Garm lahu mera khaulda ae, bringing the heat
Tu hi taan meri manzil ae, making me complete

[Bridge]
Saeed karda ae tera intezaar, looking for love
Tu hi meri jaan ae sohniye, sent from above
Naina vich nasha ae tera, pulling me close
Bullan te tera hi naam ae, my beautiful rose

[Final Chorus]
L.A.M. Fire wangu machdi ae tu, burning so bright
